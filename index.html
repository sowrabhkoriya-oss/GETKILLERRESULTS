Faq

How soon can we expect our first qualified meetings?▼
Most clients begin seeing positive replies within the first 2 weeks after launch. Qualified meetings typically start being booked within 2–4 weeks, depending on your market, offer, and sales readiness.
What do you need from our team?▼
Your team provides details on services & offers, ICP clarity, previous clients details, initial inputs on branding/brand voice, case studies, approvals of scripts and content, and mainly attends qualified sales meetings.
Do we need to hire SDRs or purchase additional software?▼
No. We provide the outreach infrastructure, software, data, and operational support required to run your campaigns. The only assets you'll need to purchase are those tied to your brand and intellectual property—such as outreach domains, outreach email inboxes, and a LinkedIn profile with Premium. There are no additional hiring costs, software subscriptions, or hidden fees.
How do you ensure the meetings are qualified?▼
Every opportunity is screened against your Ideal Customer Profile (ICP) and qualified before reaching your calendar. We focus on connecting you with decision-makers who have a genuine business need.
Can we review the messaging before campaigns go live?▼
Absolutely. Nothing is launched without your approval. You'll review the messaging, positioning, lead lists and LinkedIn content.
What happens if a campaign isn't performing?▼
We continuously optimize targeting, messaging, offers, and campaign performance using live data. Weekly reviews ensure we're always improving results instead of waiting until the end of the engagement. We work as an internal part of your company with daily check-ins and corrections to get the best results.
Is this suitable for every business?▼
Our process is designed for B2B companies with a clear offer, an identifiable Ideal Customer Profile, and a sales team capable of handling qualified meetings.
